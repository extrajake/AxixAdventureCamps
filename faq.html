FAQs

Please refer to the COVID-19 section for our guidelines and policy procedures.

What is the minimum age my child can be to start camp?
Your child must be 3 or turn 3 within the season you are registering for.
What biking abilities does Axis cater to?
We coach all abilities from balance bikers, just pedaling, to kids exploring on blue trails.
Do we need to pack snacks and lunch, and is this a nut free camp?
Yes Axis is a nut free camp. Please pack a snack in your child’s camelback and a healthy lunch in a lunch kit.
What should my child bring to camp?
Children should arrive at camp ready for biking (helmet, gloves, elbow/knee pads, camelback with water/snack). Children should be dressed for the weather as bike rain or shine! A ziplock or small backpack with a change of clothes, and water gear (swimmers, spray sunscreen, water shoes if needed). A healthy packed lunch with a sun hat attached. ​
