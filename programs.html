Our Program

Axis Adventure Camps offer full day camps and incorporate traditional activities with something a little different, guaranteeing a fun and exhilarating time. Campers will begin their day biking in either one of our balance bike groups, or by heading out to explore Whistler's trail network. Afternoons are spent participating in a range of activities including paddle boarding, balls sports, and nature exploration. Our program, which is run by coaches that you know and love, encourages campers to get out of their comfort zones and embrace new challenges, ensuring your child comes home with new knowledge and skills.
